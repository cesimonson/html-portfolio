The Best Movies According to Christine

My Top 3 movies of all-time.

Three Idiots

Inspirational Bollywood comedy about some college students and surprising outcomes in their future.

Borat

Comedy about a reporter from Kazakhstan who comes to the US and displays the most outrageous behavior and says the most un-PC things. Could be offensive to some, so movie can't be taken seriously

Life is Beautiful

Very moving story of a father and son who survive the Holocaust by using humor and positivity to get through each day.
